A quick table for Linear Reg vs Logistic Reg:

| Feature           | Linear Regression           | Logistic Regression (Classification) |
|------------------|--------------------------|------------------------------------|
| **Model**        | \( y = wx + b \)          | \( y = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(wx + b)}} \) |
| **Loss Function** | Mean Squared Error (MSE) | Cross-Entropy Loss (Binary)        |
